Temperature Sensor

Temperature is one of the most
important environmental signals for plants. High and low
temperatures have a variety
of effects that affect plant growth and
development profoundly.

Humidity Sensor

This sensor let us know the humidity in the
atmosphere and by this we can
get the reading so we can know what amount
of water our plant need.

Soil Moisture Sensor

Soil moisture is a key variable in
controlling the exchange of water and heat energy
between the land and surfaces
so plays an important role in the development
of weather patterns and the production of precipitation.
